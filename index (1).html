Our Story

Founded in 2010 by master perfumer Kloe Anderson, Kloe's Fragrance began as a passion project in a small Parisian apartment. What started as handcrafted scents for friends quickly grew into a globally recognized luxury brand.

Today, we continue Kloe's vision of creating extraordinary fragrances that evoke emotion and create lasting memories. Each bottle is a testament to our commitment to quality, sustainability, and artistic expression.

Ethical Sourcing

We partner with sustainable farms worldwide to source only the finest ingredients.

Artisan Crafted
